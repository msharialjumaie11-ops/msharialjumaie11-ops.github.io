About Me

I am a lecturer with a Master's degree from the University of Texas at San Antonio, specializing in deep learning and artificial intelligence. My academic journey has been driven by a passion for exploring cutting-edge technologies and their real-world applications. Over the years, I have authored numerous research papers in the fields of AI and machine learning, contributing to both academic and industry advancements. One of my proudest achievements includes publishing a book chapter focused on the Metaverse, where I examined its transformative potential in education and digital interaction.
